Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup of tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for innovation and collaboration.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to inspire, educate, and connect people from all corners of the tech world. We are driven by a commitment to innovation, diversity, and positive change.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace

Known as the first computer programmer. She inspired a generation of technologists with her pioneering work on Charles Babbage's Analytical Engine.

Alan Turing

A foundational figure in computer science and AI. His work on cryptography and the Turing Machine transformed the tech world.

Grace Hopper

A trailblazer in programming and the creator of the first compiler. She paved the way for modern programming languages.
